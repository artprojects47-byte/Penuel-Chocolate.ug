Our Journey

The Penuel Story

The Beginning

Our company was founded after spotting a gap in the Ugandan market that was dominated by imported chocolate products, after thorough research Penuel Chocolate was born as a Ugandan chocolate brand with a goal to bring out the best out of Uganda's cocoa beans.

Focus and Resilience

The word Penuel means "Face of God" originating from the biblical story where Jacob wrestled with an angel and saw God face-to-face at that location, we understood that the task was not easy to change the status quo in Uganda and it required determination, resilience, focus and fighting hard with all our strength to make a recognisable impact in our society.

Local Sourcing

Best chocolate comes from best cocoa beans. We source our cocoa from specific regions of Uganda that have a suitable climate and fertile soils that favour the best yield of quality cocoa, our direct corroboration with farmers ensures best farming practises that improves the quality of cocoa beans used in our chocolate production.

Educating Palates

Many people in Africa have misunderstood chocolate because the market hasn't offered enough options. We explore the different varieties of chocolate from Dark chocolate spectrum, Milk chocolate spectrum, chocolates with inclusions like nuts, dried fruits, herbs and banana chips, drinking chocolate and chocolate spreads.

Our Vision

To build a global chocolate empire that is a pillar to sustainable development through fair trade, biodiversity, job opportunities, and promoting gender-based equality in the community.
